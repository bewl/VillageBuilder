🎯 High Impact - Make It Feel Alive
1. Day/Night Cycle & Visual Feedback
•	People go home at night - Workers return to their houses/family homes at end of day
•	Building lights - Houses glow at night when occupied
•	Sleep/rest mechanics - People recover energy while sleeping
•	Daily routines - Wake up, work, eat dinner, sleep
2. Needs & Survival
•	Hunger affects work - Hungry workers are slower/less productive
•	Food consumption - Actually deduct grain when people eat (currently just tracked)
•	Starvation consequences - People get sick before dying
•	Water need - Require wells near houses
•	Warmth in winter - Firewood actually matters for survival
3. Population Growth
•	Children - Married couples have babies over time
•	Aging system - People age and eventually die of old age
•	Generational gameplay - Watch families grow over decades
•	Family traits - Skills passed down through generations
4. Economy & Trading
•	Currency system - Coins/gold from selling surplus
•	Market building - Trade resources between villages or merchants
•	Prices fluctuate - Supply and demand for resources
•	Taxes - Town Hall collects from workers
